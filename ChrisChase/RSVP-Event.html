"Artist on Go"

"Creativity is in the air - Come join us and meet celebrity stylist Ricardo Rojas"

February 07, 2019



We're excited to announce a special event here at Chris Chase Salon! Right on time for New York Fashion Week 2019, we’re inviting you to a hair styling experience organized by Artist On Go, featuring the celebrity stylist Ricardo Rojas.

Please join us for cocktails and hors-d'oeuvres, connect with industry creatives, and experience fashionable, fabulous hair styled by Ricardo Rojas. This will be a night to remember!

Here’s all the information you need to know:

What is Artist On Go?

ArtistOnGo is empowering artists with freedom and flexibility. It gives artists the freedom to book spots in the best salons, by hour or by day, with no time constraints or long term commitments - so now stylists can book appointments with their clients anywhere, anytime !

Where?

Chris Chase Salon, 182 9th Ave, NYC (between 21st and 22nd Street)

When?
