"Best Hair Colors for Fall and Winter"

November 21, 2019



The seasonal shift took us from a warm summer breeze into colder, rainy days. If you’re looking for a hair color change to spice up your look for fall and winter, you're definitely not alone. No matter which season is next, color transformations are never a bad idea, and our stylists always have you covered. Vibrant reds, shiny blondes and several shades of brunette - our talented team loves to create stunning colors for the cold months. Experimenting with colors and discovering endless options is what this season is about. See some of the beautiful looks our team already created in our West Chelsea salon and get inspired!


Bright Blonde
Brighten up your hair and shine through the upcoming season. Going blonder is always a good decision, don’t be afraid to make a bold statement this season. The stunning look you see in the picture was created by our Master Colorist Carlos. Give us a call to book your appointment with him and get your locks colored in one of the countless, beautiful, bright blonde shades!

Bronde
Can’t decide if you want to go for a warm brunette or blonde this season? Why not mix both? Our talented colorist Mike created this stunning bronde. With some warm blonde highlights he created beautiful color accents in the brunette hair of his clients with added movement and dimensions. The results are gorgeous, natural looking hair!

Pumpkin Spice
Whenever it comes to beautiful color creations, there are no limits for our Stylist Adolfo. He created a shiny, unique look for his client and got her ready for the upcoming season. This fiery red is definitely not for everyone, but if you’re looking for something that isn’t the average wallflower, it might be the perfect color for you!

Chocolate Brown
You can’t go wrong with a warm shade of brunette. Whether you’re looking for a lighter or darker look, you can ask your stylist to add some highlights in order to create a unique style and find your perfect hair color match. The beautiful creation you see in the picture was made by Aut and is a stunner for sure!

Honey Blonde
Our Hair Stylist and Colorist Lian is responsible for this gorgeous honey-toned, buttery blonde. The blonde shade complement his client's skin tone perfectly, and simply looks amazing. By using highlights, Lian added multidimensional accents and definitely got the best out of the Wella Professional Colors he used. If you want to get your locks colored as well, give us a call!

Perfect Balayage
Balayage means a special hair painting method which is perfect to create unique, natural looking hair color creations. No matter which color you want to go for, what your natural hair color is, or what type of hair you have, there are several options to create a stunning style. The color in the picture is the work of our Stylist Carlos and was created with Goldwell Colors.

Get Your Hair Colored
Are you ready to revamp your haircolor? You can find our salon in West Chelsea, not too far from New York Penn Station, and right by the Chelsea Food Market. Our professional, incredibly talented team will take care of you and your hair to achieve the look of your dreams. For an appointment, you can give us a call or use our online booking option. We’re looking forward to having you at our NYC salon!
