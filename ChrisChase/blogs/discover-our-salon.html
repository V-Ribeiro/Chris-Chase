"Discover Chris Chase Salon"

November 7, 2019



Welcome to Chris Chase Salon! We’re a luxury hair salon located in the heart of Manhattan. Since almost 30 years, our talented team has been providing the best haircuts, coloring services and treatments the city has to offer. Upon entering our salon, you’ll be greeted by a warm, welcoming, professional atmosphere. The extravagant, glamorous interior makes Chris Chase Salon unique and promises an outstanding experience. Read on and discover our West Chelsea salon!


Get Comfortable
Our waiting area will let the time pass faster than you can imagine! Sit back, relax, browse through our magazines, enjoy coffee, tea, or our free WiFi. Our spacious sofas in the lobby provide enough space for you, your friends, your family or whomever you may bring. Your luxurious experience at Chris Chase starts the second you enter our NYC salon!

Salon Area
Our salon features dark walls, a brilliant white floor, transparent chairs and a dramatic chandelier which creates an elegant and sophisticated feel. Each space is equipped with a large mirror, ensuring you can gaze your new, beautiful hairstyle. Enjoy the luxurious interior, a glass of champagne or wine and let our stylists work their magic!

Shampoo Station
At our shampoo station, you’ll discover the luxurious brands we’re working with like Goldwell and Wella. In a wide range of different shampoos and conditioners, your stylist will find the matching one for your hair type. While getting pampered, you’ll have a great view of the majestic chandelier poised in the middle of our salon!

Shop
We’re proud to offer only the finest shampoos, hair masks, conditioners, and other hair care products for our clients. Chris Chase Salon is one of the few salons exclusive with Oribe, one of the top brands in the beauty industry. We have both styling and cleaning Oribe products available for purchase inside our salon. Come by and find your favorite Oribe hair care product!

Visit Us
Visit us at Chris Chase Salon and get to know our stylists, our beautiful elegant space, and our services. We promise you an outstanding, truly pampering salon experience with the most talented and professional stylists in New York. Give us a call for your appointment or book online. We’re looking forward to welcoming you in our Chelsea salon soon.
