"Beauty Gift Ideas: Holiday Hair Care Specials"

December 3, 2019



It's that time of the year again : the holiday season is finally here! December has officially started, which means if you haven’t already bought and wrapped some christmas gifts for your loved ones, it's definitely time to get started. At Chris Chase Salon, you’ll find the perfect present for everyone on your list. We carry a wide range of high quality hair care brands, and let’s be honest: who doesn’t love to spend some self care time with luxurious products?! Ahead, you’ll find the best catches this season, for every budget. Let’s find the ultimate gift for all beauty obsession in your life!


R & Co
R & Co is the latest addition to our collection of amazing brands available at Chris Chase Salon. All of the products are designed by forward-thinking hairstylists, willing to provide innovative, excellent hair care for every type of hair. Besides this, R & Co products are formulated without parabens, sulfates, mineral oil and petrolatum. They’re vegetarian, cruelty-free, gluten-free and color-safe and therefore the perfect present for everyone looking for environment friendly hair care. Another plus : the experience of using R & Co products is outstanding. The packaging, as well as fragrances, are meant to enhance the experience of pampering yourself and evoke a special feeling, style or attitude. Just by looking at the bottle or tube, you’ll see what the product is about and get excited to try it. All of the products provide a subtle, yet sophisticated fragrance and can be used by men and women. So, who wouldn’t love to find some of these gorgeous products under the christmas tree?

Oribe
Since over 10 years, Oribe is one of the top notch brands in the beauty industry. The outstanding products are the perfect match for everyone looking for a luxurious present to give. At Chris Chase Salon, you’ll find a selection of shampoos, conditioners and styling products. There are several lines, ensuring to provide the perfect treatment for every type of hair. All of the luxurious products are unique, innovative and known for their outstanding quality and performance. Besides this, they are gluten-free, cruelty-free, color and keratin treatment safe and have UV protection for hair. Combined with Oribe’s signature fragrance and the glamorous packaging, they make the perfect hair care gift for everyone, including yourself. In order to add even more value to the luxurious products, we have a special offer for you : Purchase three full size Oribe products, get a fourth full size product for free (equal or lesser value). This is the perfect opportunity to create your own hair care gift set!
