ABOUT
OUR SALON

Upon entering Chris Chase, one is transported by the spaces opulent yet inviting design.

The 2,000 square foot interior features deep plum walls, brilliant white floors and luxe details provide a soothing backdrop for chic mid-century style furniture. At the center of this well-appointed interior hands a jaw dropping, dramatic crystal chandelier that gives the salon the elegant and sophisticated feel of a European atelier.

More impressive than its stunning decor is the salon’s unique pedigree: Chris Chase was conceived by the founders of highly acclaimed West Village salon Robert Kree, who sought to create a new salon experience that would raise the bar to an even higher level. Pioneering the burgeoning West Chelsea neighborhood in much the same way they did 15 years prior with Robert Kree – in a then quiet section of the now trendy West Village – Chris Chase is destined to be even more influential.

Each gifted beauty professional at Chris Chase has been handpicked for their skill and high level of education. All possess a genuine desire to meet the individual needs of each client while providing a truly pampering, luxurious salon experience. This personalized approach adds a special touch to every salon service offered at Chris Chase, from trend setting precision cuts and up to the second hair color, to Keratin smoothing treatments and everything else hair related.

Chris Chase is proud to offer only the finest salon products for your hair. We are one of the few salons exclusive with Oribe, the most luxurious hair care on the market.

Please enjoy a glass of wine or champagne with your service and you will leave Chris Chase feeling utterly transported, calmer, and more beautiful.
